Description

Pizza with mushrooms

This dish is a simple pizza recipe with topping choice of mushrooms. This recipe will show how to make the dough and how to prepare the pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups (355 ml) warm water (105°F-115°F)
  • 1 package (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 3 3/4 cups (490 g) bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil (omit if cooking pizza in a wood-fired pizza oven)
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • olive oil
  • cheese of your choice
  • thinly sliced mushrooms
  • tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Proof the yeast
  2. Make and knead the dough
  3. Let the dough rise
  4. Preheat oven
  5. Divide dough into two balls
  6. Prep the toppings
  7. Stretch out the dough until it's long and round
  8. Spread the tomato sauce evenly and start adding the cheese and mushrooms
  9. Add the pizza to the oven and wait till the crust is browned (around 15 minutes)
