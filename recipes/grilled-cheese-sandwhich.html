Grilled Cheese

This dish is a simple grilled cheese sandwhich. It is quite easy to make and can be eaten during any time of the day.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Cheese of your choice
  • Two slices of bread

Steps

  1. Start heating your pan to medium-high heat
  2. Spread butter evenly against one side of each slice of bread
  3. Next add the cheese of your choice between the unbuttered side of the sliced bread, forming a sandwhich
  4. Add your sandwhich to the hot pan and flip till golden brown on the side faced down
  5. Take the grilled cheese sandwhich off the pan and enjoy!
